Magazine Layout Design

Supper Moment

This is a magazine layout design assessment I did for Hong Kong band Supper Moment's album '19 Moment' in my design, I embraced their album theme of
家會為你開，永遠為你等待。(A welcoming home that's always open for you.)
Using a warm color scheme, I design a layout that exudes comfort and familiarity. Through thoughtful typography and imagery, I aimed to evoke the feeling of being at home, inviting readers to experience a sense of belonging and nostalgia as they navigate the pages.
