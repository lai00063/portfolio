Taiwan Bird The Voice of Nature

There are varieties of living species in Taiwan, many of which are at the risk of extinction. Among them, the numbers of birds are gradually decreasing due to environmental changes, habitat destruction, illegal hunting, trading, etc. We had chosen 9 types of endemic species of bird in Taiwan that are on the verge of extinction, recreating their chirping sound so that people can hear its euphonies rhythm.
